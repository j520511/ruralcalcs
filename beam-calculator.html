Canadian Building Code Notes

The NBC (National Building Code of Canada) requires beams to be sized by a qualified designer for loads outside of prescriptive tables. The NBC Part 9 span tables cover most house framing; use them as your primary reference for code compliance.

  • Deflection limit: L/360 for floors, L/240 for roofs
  • Snow loads vary significantly by region (reference NBC Appendix C or local authority)
  • Always check with your local building department before construction
